FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

When my account will be ready?

Well, it depends from the type of the account. For exaple, if you are requesting a podcast guest account before the release of the episode, the account will be ready after the release of the episode. In general, an account to be created, it takes up to 10 days according to the complexity of data visualization etc. You will receive an email from us (notifications@app.changemakerz.org) once the account is set up.

The portal is mobile friendly?

Yes, it's created based on UI experience. Just login from your mobile device and you are ready to go.

How is this free?

The ChangemakerZ Portal, has been created for marketers from marketers and it help all the teams to achieve their goals. Since this system doesn't require extra resources, the maintenace and distribution is covered from our collaborator - IBN Cloud by Vasilis Skarleas. No costs to cover, free for everyone!
